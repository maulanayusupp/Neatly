---

Neatly is a growing toolbox for the little jobs developers do every day —
beautify a blob of minified JS, decode a JWT, convert JSON to YAML, explain a
cron expression, generate a QR code. One clean, fast home for all of them.

## Why you'll keep it open

- 🔒 **Private by default** — most tools run entirely in your browser. Your data isn't uploaded or stored.
- ⚡ **Instant** — paste, get your result, move on. No spinners, no waiting.
- 🆓 **Free & open** — no account, no paywall, no tracking-for-ads.
- 🌍 **Five languages** — English, Bahasa Indonesia, Bahasa Melayu, Filipino, Tiếng Việt.
- 📲 **Installable** — add it to your desktop or phone and use it offline.
